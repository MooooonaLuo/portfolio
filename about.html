WORK EXPERIMENTS ABOUT

Hi! I'm Mona Luo.

I'm a product designer who crafts meaningful experiences with curiosity, empathy, and creativity. Coming from a background in new media arts, I have both an artist's sensibility and a designer's rationality.

I studied new media art at the University of California, San Diego, during my undergraduate years. This experience empowered me to have an artistic and humane perspective on human-computer interaction. With a desire to dive deeper into product design, I embarked on a new journey studying in the MFA Interaction Design program at the School of Visual Arts in September 2021.

When I'm not doing design, I enjoy taking photos and watching movies.
